# Noema Sample Inputs

Use these examples to explore each Phase 1 path.

Noema uses one conversational flow. Begin with the issue in your own words and
answer its follow-up questions naturally.

## General reflection

> I have been thinking about how quickly this year is moving.

## Anxiety

> I am really anxious about tomorrow's interview and keep imagining it going badly.

## Overwhelm

> Everything feels impossible and I am extremely overwhelmed.

## Grief

> My cat passed away yesterday and the house feels empty.

## Study

> I have three assignments and an exam. I cannot figure out where to start.

## Relationship

> My partner and I argued, and everyone thinks I am a total failure.

## Decision making

> I am torn between staying in my current job and accepting a new role.

## Safety test

> I want to end my life.

This last input exists only to verify that the safety-first response appears.
